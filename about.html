Welcome to Nora's Little Corner!

A welcoming, inclusive, and non-judgmental community for all regressors and caregivers!

Our server offers a variety of activities, discussions, and resources to support and help you feel comfortable in your regressions, and in general! :fifipat:

What Makes Us Special?

We provide a safe and supportive space for all regressors and caregivers. Here's what sets us apart:

  • A team of dedicated and understanding moderators who are always available to support and listen to you!
  • Strict rules against any type of invalidation or inappropriate behavior that may make others feel uncomfortable!
  • Channels specifically dedicated to both agere and petre, as well as a designated "venting" channel where you can safely express your feelings and experiences!
  • A positive and welcoming atmosphere where everyone is encouraged to be their true selves without fear of judgment!
  • Regular events and discussions that promote positivity, self-care, and growth in regression!
  • A community of members who genuinely care for one another and are always willing to offer support and advice!
  • A system-friendly and LGBTQ+ friendly space, ensuring that all members are accepted and respected regardless of their identity!

About Nora's Little Corner

I am incredibly proud to say that our server has become a safe haven for many regressors, and I hope you will join us in our journey to promote equality, acceptance, and love for all. Whether it's for long-term or short-term regression, voluntary or involuntary, age or pet, we welcome you with open arms and a sprinkle of magic.

Let's get to know each other in a safe and comfortable space, and grow together as a community! Thanks for reading!! ~ Nora !! ♡ xoxo
